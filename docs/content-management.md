# Content Management

Use the configured admin route, `/admin` by default, to manage:

- Pages and flexible blocks
- Services and industries
- Navigation menus
- Forms and lead submissions
- Testimonials, case studies, FAQs, and resources
- SEO metadata and redirects
- Site settings and branding

Starter content is editable. Draft posts, draft case studies, and draft testimonials are intentionally not public until reviewed and published.

## Visual Composer workflow

Use **Website > Visual Composer** for page-level editing support:

1. Select a page from the page selector.
2. Click a section in the live preview or in Page structure.
3. Review Publishing checklist, Social preview, and Block QA before saving.
4. Use Content, Design, Media, Responsive, and Advanced tabs for controlled block updates.
5. Use Quick page settings for page title, publish status, SEO metadata, social images, sitemap, schema, and noindex/nofollow checks.
6. Save the selected block or page settings, then refresh or switch viewport to verify desktop, tablet, and mobile.

The Composer is a safe editing layer over Payload page blocks. It does not replace the normal Payload editor; use **Advanced edit** when you need full SEO, status, slug, or relationship fields.

Before publishing a page, verify:

- The page is published only when content is ready.
- SEO title and meta description are configured.
- Open Graph or Twitter image is configured.
- The page has visible sections, a main hero/title, and at least one CTA or contact section.
- Images have useful alt text and reasonable file sizes.
